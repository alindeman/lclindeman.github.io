I spent two formative high-school years at The Mississippi School for Mathematics and Science before attending Birmingham-Southern College, where I graudated summa cum laude with a BA in English. Since then, I've worked as a non-profit professional in Huntsville, AL and now in Atlanta, GA. I've assisted with fundraising events and handled all aspects of donation management, from entering gifts in Raiser's Edge to drafting and mailing acknowledgment letters. I also pay the bills, order copious amounts of office supplies and coffee, stock the food pantry, and keep the building standing by coordinating facilities maintenance needs. I've had the opportunity to help launch my organization's social media presence, managing our Twitter account and brainstorming new campaigns to enhance our online fundraising efforts.

Offline I enjoy hiking and backpacking with my husband. The kitchen is my happy place, and I'm always trying out new recipes that I find on Pinterest. I'm a firm proponent of the Oxford comma and somewhat pedantic about the conventions of the 4-way stop. I seem to collect funky earrings and cardigans. I enjoy monthly meetings with my book club, where we sometimes talk about the book we've read (anything from pop fiction to literature to non-fiction!) but also drink wine and eat lots of cheese. You can usually find me at Rails Girls on the last Wednesday of the month.
